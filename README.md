📊 Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI
📌 Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI. It provides a comprehensive view of sales trends, growth metrics, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

Analyze sales performance across different business segments
Track monthly trends and year-over-year (YoY) growth
Monitor cumulative sales (YTD)
Identify top-performing categories and potential areas for improvement
📈 Key Features
🔹 Sales by Category

Visual comparison of total sales across:

Novelty Shop
Supermarket
Computer Store
Corporate
Gift Store

Quickly identifies top revenue drivers.

🔹 Monthly Sales Performance Table

Displays:

Total Sales by Month
Year-over-Year Growth (%)
Year-to-Date (YTD) Sales

Helps track performance trends and seasonality.

🔹 Quantity Trend Analysis

Line chart showing Sum of Quantity by Month highlights fluctuations in demand throughout the year.

🔹 Geographic Analysis

Map visualization of Sales by Province provides regional insights and distribution patterns.

🔹 KPI Indicator

Displays total Sum of Quantity (3M) for a quick performance snapshot.

🔹 Year Filter

Interactive slicer allowing users to filter the report by year (e.g., 2019).

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Microsoft Power BI
Power Query (Data Transformation)
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
📊 Key Insights
Novelty Shop is the top-performing category (~$36M in sales), significantly outperforming others.
Sales show strong growth in February (25%), August (16.78%), and December (20.01%).
Slight negative or flat growth observed in March (-0.21%), indicating potential performance issues.
Peak sales periods occur mid-year and at year-end, suggesting seasonal trends.
Quantity trends fluctuate, with noticeable dips and recoveries throughout the year.
